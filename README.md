# Scheme-Finder-Bot
🏘️ Scheme Finder Bot
1M1B AI for Sustainability Virtual Internship | IBM SkillsBuild & AICTE
A conversational AI chatbot that helps every Indian — rural or urban — discover government welfare schemes they are eligible for, instantly and for free.

Problem Statement
Millions of people across India — both in villages and cities — are unaware of government schemes they qualify for. Complex paperwork, language barriers, and lack of awareness prevent them from getting benefits like free housing, health insurance, scholarships, and farm support.

How might we use AI to help every Indian discover government welfare schemes so that poverty and inequality can be reduced?


SDG Alignment

SDG 1 — No Poverty — Connects families to cash transfer schemes
SDG 3 — Good Health — Surfaces health schemes like Ayushman Bharat
SDG 4 — Quality Education — Shows scholarships for students
SDG 10 — Reduced Inequalities — Makes government info accessible to everyone


 Features

Chat in simple English about your situation
Fill your profile and get matched schemes instantly
See full details — benefit amount, how to apply, documents needed
Works completely offline — no internet required
Mobile friendly


 Technologies Used

HTML — structure
CSS — design and animations
JavaScript — bot logic and scheme database
